---
title: Giving Component Parts Identity
description: Reusable component parts eventually need stable styling identity.
---

A reusable component usually has more than one part.

A `Card` has a root, title, body, actions, maybe a badge.

Another team may want to style one of those parts without learning whether the
title is an `h2`, a `span`, or wrapped in another `div`.

If those parts do not have names, teams reach for whatever is available.

Generated classes.

DOM selectors.

Extra props.

Those can work, but they are not always stable contracts.

A slot gives the component part a stable styling name:

```txt
Button.root
Button.icon
Button.label
```

Once the part has identity, themes and debugging have something stable to
refer to.

With identity in place, outside styling has something stable to refer to
without depending on the component's DOM shape.
